About Us

MyHoop is a streamlined mobile app that connects basketball players and facilitates the organization of games and events, making it easy to find and enjoy local hoops action.

What We Offer

MyHoop facilitates community and connection through sports
MyHoop allows events such as tournaments, fundraisers, etc to be organized
MyHoop has a sleek user interface for all types of people to use
MyHoop promotes a healthy lifestyle for individuals of all ages and sizes

MyHoop's mission is to unite hoopers alike throughout local communities and use sports to bring people together

-MyHoop Team
